Kyber crystals are used for lightsabers, the kyber krystals act as the energy to produce the light from the saber. With these kyber krystals their exist multiple colors and each color has a different meaning.
  • Red is commonly used for the sith a common way for the sith to create their kyber krystal is to, take a jedi's krystal and bleed it red hence its red color
  • Blue is common within the jedi blue stands for a jedi guardian a person who used the more physical aspect of the force.
  • Unlike the previous color blue green represents one more intuned with the spirutal side of the force
  • Orange krystals are much weaker than other krystals this crystal represents a jedi who doesn't believe in violence unless absolutely necsercary hence why this type of krystal/lightsaber was weaker than the others.
  • White krystals represent a purified krystal if you remeber what was said about the red krystal and how its bleeded to become red the white crystal is what happens when someone purifies a red krystal its purifacation becomes white.
  • Yellow is pretty simple it stand for someone with lost identity, this color is most commonly seen with jedi temple guards.
  • The black krystal also known as darksaber is rare with only one existing the darksaber can read the emotions of its user and can be very powerful in the hands of someone mentally strong but in the hands of someone weak it's pretty much usless. A very fun fact is that whoever holds the darksaber controls mandalore.
